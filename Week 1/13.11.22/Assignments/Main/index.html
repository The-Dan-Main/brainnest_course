Introduction

I remember as if it were yesterday when our summer vacation was ruined by a strong storm and a deluge of rain. We were camping by a lake and had to stay in our tent for three days.
My mother unwrapped a tape recorder and a gift that was still wrapped. I was five years old at the time and had always been fascinated by magic and stories about magicians.
Inside the gift box was the German version of the audiobook of "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" on six cassettes.
We three children were so enthralled by the story that we listened to it directly three times in a row.

Since then, I was in love with the world of Harry Potter and read every book, watch every movie and seeked every insight of it.
Years later, I bought all seven audiobooks and still listen to them when I can't sleep or on a rainy Sunday morning.
My brother and I can reenact all seven audiobooks almost by heart and know details about this magical world that few know.

This year is was able to visit the "Making of Harry Potter - Warner Brother Studios" in Leavesden, UK and found a ton of further insights.
As a true fan, I would love to share this knowledge with you and I truly hope you enjoy those hidden gems in this magical wizardry world!
